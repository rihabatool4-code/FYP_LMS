About GGCW Library

Government Graduate College Women has been at the forefront of women's education since its establishment. Our library serves as the intellectual heart of our institution, fostering learning and research.

Our Mission

The GGCW Library is committed to empowering women through education by providing comprehensive access to knowledge resources, modern learning facilities and innovative digital solutions.

  • Academic Excellence – Supporting students with comprehensive resources
  • Digital Innovation – Technology integration for seamless library management
  • Inclusive Environment – Welcoming space for all students
